Student Counselor System

Student Counselor System is a vision created by four students eager to create a platform where the interaction between teachers and students is improved and facilitated by the newest technology. It aims to eradicate the manual and often repetitive work involved in producing reports, certificates and attendance sheets with the help of this website and mobile application.This automation of work leads to better use of human resources.

We strongly believe in Knowledge is power and that such power must be accessible to everyone without any barriers.The purpose of this system is to make this reality come true by extending it's functionalities to provide latest information,post,articles to students to expand their knowledge horizon and make them more aware of the new world and the oppurtunities it holds for them.
